TestTafel

TestTafel is a restaurant that pops up every weekend to serve a 7 course meal (including amuses, bread, and coffee bites). Our menu is an eclectic collection of recipes we have been wanting to make for years, dishes stolen from our favourite chefs, and recipes found in obscure cookbooks. With dinner we serve cocktails, homemade lemonades, and our favourite cheapy wines.

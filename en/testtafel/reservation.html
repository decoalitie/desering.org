Opening time

TestTafel opens at 19:00. We kindly ask you to arrive between 19:00 and 19:15. If you really cannot make that time, please get in touch with us at testtafel@desering.org after you book a reservation to discuss alternative starting times.

Which table would you like to join?

You can reserve a private table if you reserve for 5 or more people. Smaller groups can join our large shared table.

COVID-19 tests

Each visitor of TestTafel has to show an official corona proof from the CoronaCheck app or bring a physical negative self-test. Alternatively, we sell tests at the door for 2 euros a piece.

Contact info

We will contact you by phone if we have questions about your reservation (for example, about allergies) or to message you when a spot opens up for our waiting list.

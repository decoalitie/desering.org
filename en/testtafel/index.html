TestTafel

TestTafel is a restaurant that pops up every Saturday and Sunday to serve a 7 course meal (including amuses, bread, and coffee bites) for just €20. Our menu is an eclectic collection of recipes we have been wanting to make for years, dishes stolen from our favourite chefs, and recipes found in obscure cookbooks.

TestTafel is moving to De Sloot!

This spring we will re-open TestTafel at a new location: De Sloot. With a giant 300m2 hall and a 200m2 terrace we can bring our restaurant to the next level. There will also be enough space for our People's Kitchen De Sering and all their activities. While we are working hard to prepare this space to welcome our new guests, our restaurant is unfortunately closed.

We need your support!

We started a crowdfund campaign for our starting budget through the platform CrowdAboutNow. You can contribute through small loans or by buying credit that you can spend in our restaurant once we are open. More information can be found on our crowdfund page.

If you have friends or family that might be interested and able to help us out, sharing our crowdfund page, promo video or Instagram page is greatly appreciated!

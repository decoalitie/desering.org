Food and drinks

On Tuesday through Friday you are welcome to join us for staff dinner! What once started as the meal for our own staff is now open to everyone. For only €4.50 you get a vegan soup and main course.

  • Lunch The kitchen is open between 13:00 and 14:00. After this time, we may run out of food or no longer serve any.
  • Dinner We serve dinner between 18:00 and 19:00. After this time, we may run out of food or no longer serve any.

People's Kitchen

Every Tuesday we open our People's Kitchen: the dinner for which De Sering was originally founded. Everyone is welcome to have two vegan courses for a suggested donation of €2.50-€5.00.

The People's Kitchen serves food between tussen 18:00 en 22:00

TestTafel

Every Friday and Saturday the experimental restaurant TestTafel serves a seven-course dinner at De Sering. Reservations are required to attend this dinner.

TestTafel is a non-profit foundation. By having dinner at TestTafel you are supporting the activities of De Sering!

Go to testtafel.nl for more information.

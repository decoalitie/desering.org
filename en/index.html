We have moved!

As some of you may already know, we are moving De Sering to our own location. You can read more about it further down this page. We are still busy finalizing the space and our kitchen. In May we will have an official opening party. We will update you about that soon.

However, despite not everything being 100% finished, the space is ready for us to host our People's Kitchen!

From now on, we will open on Tuesdays.
The new address is: Rhôneweg 6, 1043AH Amsterdam

People's Kitchen De Sering

Every Tuesday we serve two vegan courses for a suggested donation of €2.50-€5.00. Everyone is welcome between 18:30 and 22:00 at Rhôneweg 6.

It's no longer necessary to make a reservation. Please feel free to drop by!

De Sloot: a new home for our people's kitchen

De Sering is moving to a new location, along with our pop-up restaurant TestTafel. We will have our own space at De Sloot: the largest theatre breeding space in The Netherlands. We will have enough space to do everything we ever dreamed of: a massive 300m2 hall and 200m2 terrace.

We started a crowdfund through the platform CrowdAboutNow for our starting budget. This can be done through small loans or by buying credits in our presale that you can spend at our new location once we open.

This crowdfund has reached 100% of our minimum funding goal! This gives us enough money for everything that's strictly necessary for us to open. It makes it possible for us to host The People's Kitchen from this new location from April 12th onwards. Many thanks to everyone who helped us reaching this goal!

However, we are not done yet: to completely finish up the space as we envisioned, we need the crowdfund to gather another €60.000. This allows us to open in a way that truly utilizes this awesome space.

So if you have friends or family that might be interested and able to help us out, sharing our crowdfund page, promo video or Instagram page is greatly appreciated!

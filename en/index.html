People's Kitchen De Sering

Coming together for dinner every Tuesdays and Thursdays, for €2,50. Everyone is welcome starting from 18:30 and the kitchen stays open till 21:00. The food is vegetarian, and there is always a vegan option available.

Location

The location is the Middenweg 22bg in Amsterdam-East. We leave the door open – you have to go through a tunnel and then you enter the garden. In the back is the building of de Sering. Be careful not to make any noise in the garden! Our neighbors can hear everything, and we like to stay on friendly terms with them.

Reservations

We reach people through various platforms and we need some indication of attendance. At this time we have a maximum capacity of 40 people. Reserve a spot to be sure you can join for dinner!

What is the relation to de Coalitie

De Sering originally started as an initiative of the student party de Coalitie (the Coalition), and is open to everyone, regardless of political interest. We started it because we believe there should be more spaces where people can come together in an informal setting. And where there is good and cheap food.

Our people's kitchen is not a political gathering, but it is in line with the ideas of de Coalitie: de Coalitie is working for more connectedness and involvement with and of students. Eating together is one expression of that.

Also, we regulary host events and talks, among others by Extinction Rebellion. Would you like to organise something yourself? Send us a mail, and we can find out if we are a match!

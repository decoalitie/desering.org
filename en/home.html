De Sloot: a new home for our people's kitchen

De Sering is moving to a new location, along with our pop-up restaurant TestTafel. This spring our people's kitchen will re-open at De Sloot: the largest theatre breeding space in The Netherlands. We will have enough space to do everything we ever dreamed of: a massive 300m2 hall and 200m2 terrace.

In order to make this happen we need your help! We are starting a crowdfund through the platform CrowdAboutNow to for our starting budget. This can be done through small loans or by buying credits in our presale that you can spend at our new location once we open. You can find more details on the crowdfund page through the button below.

If you have friends or family that might be interested and able to help us out, sharing our crowdfund page, promo video or Instagram page is greatly appreciated!

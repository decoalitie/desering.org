Use our space!

De Sering's goal is to create public space. This means that you can use the space for activities and events with a public function. Whether you want to organise a dance night, a meeting with your book club, or you want to run your own pop-up restaurant from our kitchen for an evening: the possibilities are endless! We strive to keep this as cheap as possible (and in many cases even completely free!)

Do you have an idea for an activity? We'd love to talk to you! You can reach us at info@desering.org. info@desering.org.

Rental

You can also rent our space for other types of events. Think for example of a private wedding party, a private dinner or a corporate event.

The costs are determined in consultation and depend on what your event needs. We work with a turnover guarantee or a fixed rental price depending on the type of event.

For more information please send an email to info@testtafel.nl. You can help us by being as clear as possible about what you are looking for, and at least include the following information:

  • Date
  • Times
  • Number of people
  • Type of event

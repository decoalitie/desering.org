Casting theater dinner

For Toneelgroep Amsterdam's play Casting we serve a three-course menu (plus bread) for between 25 and 35 euros.

The theater dinner always starts at 19:30. On Wednesday and Thursday you are welcome from 17:00 for a drink, and on Friday and Saturday the terrace will be open from 13:00. You can make your reservation through this form (dutch)
